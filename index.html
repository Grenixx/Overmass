🪐 About the Game

Overmass is an experimental multiplayer arena game set in space. Players control cosmic entities that grow by absorbing mass, collide with opponents, and aim to dominate the galactic arena.

🌌 Features

  • Online multiplayer with unique IDs
  • Mass-based gameplay: bigger means stronger
  • Dynamic minimap to track other players
  • Custom naming via in-game chat (type /rename)

🎮 How to Play

Move your entity, avoid enemies until you're large enough to engage, and grow by collecting cosmic matter. Use the chat box to interact or rename your player identity.
